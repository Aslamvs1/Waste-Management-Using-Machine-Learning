Waste Segregation Tips

Recyclables

Clean plastic bottles, paper, cardboard, glass, and metal cans. Rinse containers before recycling to prevent contamination.

Organic Waste

Food scraps, garden trimmings, and biodegradable items. These can be composted to create nutrient-rich soil for plants.

Hazardous Waste

Batteries, electronics, chemicals, and medications require special disposal. Check local facilities for safe disposal options.

Landfill Waste

Non-recyclable plastics, contaminated materials, and other non-hazardous waste that cannot be recycled or composted.
